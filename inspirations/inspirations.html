These are some of the artists that inspire me:

Soviet Logos

Soviet Logos is an Instagram page I found recently and their work has helped me a lot with finding inspiration for my own work. Their aesthetic and focus on simplicity also resonates with how I like my own design work to look like.


Andrew Prahlow

Andrew Prahlow is a musician I discovered through a video game called "Outer Wilds", and have been listening to their music for about 3 years now, I find their music to be comforting and perfect to listen in the background while working on other things.


Alasdair Willson

Alasdair Willson is a motion graphics designer I don't know very much about but I do admire their work a lot, the old school mecha anime aesthetic and cyberpunk themes really resonate with me.
